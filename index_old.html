Our Chef


Inspired by her doting mother at an age of 13, Jigna developed strong liking towards food making. Her mother’s dedication and constant mentoring helped Jigna to develop her cooking skills and make variety of cuisines. She loved surprising her mother by making mouth watering cusines and getting her praises in return.Her talent was recognized and admired by her friends and extended family. Her passion knew no bounds as Jigna started improvising and made meals for large family gathering and community parties.

Feeding people and seeing the happiness on their faces, brought immense joy and a sense of completeness to her. It took years of soul searching to realize,she must take her craft to people and follow it for rest of her life. Today, through unconditional support and motivation Jigna is fulfilling her childhood dreams of having her own restaurant - Desi Tadka. She looks forward each day to add health and spice, one smile at a time.

Jigna Patel
Head Chef/Owner
